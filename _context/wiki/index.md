## Quick Summary

A personal React web app for composing chord progressions on a grid, with scale-aware harmony highlighting, piano keyboard interaction, multi-style audio playback via Tone.js, a track arranger, and MIDI export. Published at [github.com/JulienCarnec/chords-progressions-editor](https://github.com/JulienCarnec/chords-progressions-editor).
